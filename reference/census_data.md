# Sample data for census counts

A data set of invented census data.

## Usage

``` r
census_data
```

## Format

A tibble with 2 rows and 5 columns:

- census_year:

  The year of the census survey

- census_month:

  The month of the census survey

- census_day:

  The day of the census survey

- census:

  Estimated census count

- census_cv:

  Coefficient of variation (standard deviation / estimate) of census
  estimate
